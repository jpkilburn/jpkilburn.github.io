About Me

I'm a web designer from Florence, Alabama, and I love the web. I've always had an interest in technology and art, and my favorite blend of these two has been designing websites and applications. At the moment I am unemployed and looking to enter the field either under regular employment or with freelance work.

I believe in making sites with a strong focus on accessibility. I always attempt to make my designs with the user's experience in mind, so that my client's message gets to their audience as cleanly as possible.

When I'm not sifting through code I enjoy gaming, digital art, creative writing, and making music with my violin.
